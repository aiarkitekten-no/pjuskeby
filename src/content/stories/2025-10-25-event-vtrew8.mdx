Franklin was on a mission, but not the kind you would expect from a retired pudding scientist.

The Waffle Oracle wasn't on any map of Pjuskeby, but the card insisted it was located on Gnomewobble Rise. Naturally, folks were intrigued. A waffle that could tell fortunes? Who could resist?
